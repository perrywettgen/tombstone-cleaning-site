FAQ

Is your process safe for historic stones?

Yes. We use gentle hand tools and low‑pressure rinsing. We avoid acids, harsh bleach, and aggressive abrasion.

Do you handle leveling or repairs?

We can perform light leveling/straightening where permitted. Major resets or repairs may require cemetery approval.

What areas do you serve?

Based in Mount Pleasant, PA. Typical range includes Westmoreland & Fayette counties. Travel fees may apply beyond ~15 miles.

How do I prepare?

Share cemetery and plot details. We’ll confirm permissions if required and keep you updated with photos on service day.
